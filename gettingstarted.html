lets get started

Select a Suitable Location: Choose a spot for your garden that receives adequate sunlight. Most plants require at least 6-8 hours of direct sunlight daily. Ensure the location has good drainage to prevent waterlogged soil. Decide on Garden Type: Determine the type of garden you want to create, such as a flower garden, vegetable garden, herb garden, or a mix of these. Your choice will influence plant selection. Prepare the Soil: Test your soil to understand its pH and nutrient levels. You can buy a soil testing kit or send a sample to a local agricultural extension office.
